Chicken Alfredo

Savor the creamy goodness of homemade chicken Alfredo pasta. An irresistible blend of tender chicken, velvety Alfredo sauce, and vibrant broccoli florets, this makes a perfect meal for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • One whole chicken breast, boneless and sliced
  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Chicken seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Add broccoli when pasta is al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season chicken and heat olive oil on a large skillet over high heat. Cook chicken until golden brown on both sides. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. Saute minced garlic until fragrant in the skillet. Add heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese and stir until sauce is smooth and creamy.
  4. Combine pasta, broccoli, and cooked chicken with the Alfredo sauce. Toss until everything is well coated. Enjoy!
