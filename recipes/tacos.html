Tacos

Indulge in the authentic Mexican cuisine with these street style tacos. Thin-sliced grilled and marinated steak, warm fire tortillas, and fresh onions with cilantro make these tacos a staple dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank or skirt steak
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup of fresh salsa
  • Fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Mix soy sauce, olive oil, minced garlic, cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a bowl to create the marinade.
  2. Place the steak in a resealable plastic bag, pour in the marinade, seal the bag, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill or grill pan over high heat. Grill the marinated steak for 4-6 minutes per side, or until cooked to your desired doneness.
  4. Rest steak for a few minutes before slicing it thinly. Place steak on tortillas, top with salsa, onions, and cilantro. Enjoy!
